Before You Pick Up the Phone

Quick Answers to Common Boca Plumbing Questions

These are the questions our office hears every day. If yours isn't on the list, send a quick note via the contact form and a real person will reply within the hour.

What counts as a true plumbing emergency?

Anything that's flooding, spraying, leaking onto electrical, backing up into your home, or leaving you without water or hot water for more than a few hours. If you're not sure, call — we'll talk through it on the phone before sending anyone, so you don't pay for a truck roll you didn't actually need.

Do you give free phone estimates?

For most common issues, yes — we can give a realistic ballpark range based on photos or a quick description. For anything involving slab work, repipes, or full system replacements, we prefer a no-pressure on-site visit so the written quote is actually accurate, not a guess we revise later.

Will the same technician come back if I have follow-up work?

Whenever possible, yes. Continuity matters, especially on larger projects like repipes or bathroom remodels. Just mention the prior tech's name when you call and dispatch will route them back if their schedule allows.

How do I know I'm getting a fair price?

Two things: every quote is in writing before work starts, and we're happy to be a second opinion on someone else's estimate. If their number is reasonable, we'll tell you. If they're way off market, we'll tell you that too — even if it means we don't get the job.

Are you available on weekends and holidays?

Yes — 365 days a year, including Christmas Day and major hurricane recoveries. Our 24/7 emergency line is staffed by real Boca Raton dispatchers, and our on-call techs work in rotating shifts so someone is always close enough to respond within the hour.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

We do. They're optional and not pushed on first-time customers, but if you've got an older home, multiple bathrooms, or a tankless system that benefits from yearly flushing, our maintenance plans quietly save most customers more than they cost. Ask at the end of any service visit if you're curious.
